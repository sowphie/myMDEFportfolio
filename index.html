About me

A policy enthusiast, willing to find more creative approaches to resolving 21st century problems. I want to explore the intersection of design and policy: how can these two types of interventions co-create solutions to current and future problems, how can they work together and not against each other, how can design methodologies be integrated in governance, how can future-oriented design inform foresight policymaking? But first... let me understand what design is all about through this MDEF journey!
