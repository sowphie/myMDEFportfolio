About My Project


My project aims to visualise future urban policy scenarios through generative AI and foresight methodology to encourage more experimental policymaking. By simulating immersive experiences of possible futures resulting from policy initiatives, I want to provide people, and especially policymakers, with tangible and innovative tools to experience the possible impact of policies and improve civic engagement mechanisms.

Having worked in the field of EU policy beforehand, I noticed how policymakers often lack tangible evidence to make well-informed decisions based on the impact that their decisions could have. I saw the generative AI boom as a real opportunity to bring more innovative foresight tools to policymaking as AI can help us imagine scenarios that we can't fathom on our own. Such immersive experiences of possible future urban scenarios could also help policymakers gather feedback from civil society and encourage more civic participation by making policy scenarios more accessible to the wider public.

My project aims to embody a forward-thinking approach to governance, one that values innovation, inclusivity, and the collective imagination in shaping the cities of tomorrow. It’s a bold step towards a future where technology empowers democracy and fosters a deeper connection between policymakers and the communities they serve.
