Purpose Of My Project

Stakeholder Radar

Mapping and engaging with stakeholders is crucial to access resources and incorporate diverse perspectives, ultimately enhancing project legitimacy and impact. By involving stakeholders early on, I will build strong partnerships to hopefully gather enough support for my project to thrive. Drawing on my EU policy background and existing connections in this sphere, I've selected stakeholders across sectors:

Government engagement ensures policy alignment and funding opportunities. Research collaboration enriches the project with cutting-edge insights. Partnerships with industry leaders provide expertise and sponsorship opportunities. Involvement of civil society ensures ethical considerations are taken into account. Engagement with community platforms fosters grassroots participation. Collaboration with artists adds a creative dimension, reaching broader audiences. Media partnerships amplify project visibility. Investor engagement provides crucial financial support.

Strategic engagement with these different types of audiences will help me spark meaningful discussions and interventions at the intersection of AI, technology, and human identity.
